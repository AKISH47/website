About Elite Glo

Elite Glo is a young, dynamic automotive care company founded by ambitious teenagers who are passionate about delivering exceptional car washing and detailing services.

Despite our young age, our team brings fresh energy, attention to detail, and a commitment to excellence that rivals established companies. We combine modern techniques with eco-friendly products to ensure your vehicle receives the best care possible.

Our mission is to provide professional, reliable, and affordable automotive care services while building a sustainable business that serves our community with pride and integrity.
